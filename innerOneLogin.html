Logging into your account for the first time? Learn how to setup your account password.

First time login

  • Step 1

    Find your Welcome letter email and click on the activation link

  • Step 2

    Inside the input box, enter your date of birth in MM/DD/YYYY format. Click “Submit”

  • Step 3

    After entering your birthday successfully, you will see a screen showing your account has been activated. Now you need to set your password.

  • Step 4

    Check your personal email for an email from “OneLogin”. Inside, find and click on the activation link to set your password.

  • Step 5

    Enter your new password into the “New Password” and “Confirm Password”. Click “Submit”. Your new password must be at least 8 characters long, contains letters, and digits

  • Step 6

    Sign into the mySMCCD Portal using your username and password
